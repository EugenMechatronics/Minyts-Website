About Minyts

Minyts Holdings LLC is the parent structure that organizes ventures into a clean ecosystem. The goal is simple: build companies that work together like a well-tuned operating system, not a pile of disconnected tools.

Think of Minyts as the “root folder” for everything we build: shared standards, shared direction, and a consistent way to launch new products without reinventing the wheel each time.
